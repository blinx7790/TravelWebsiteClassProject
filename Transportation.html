Transportation

Rental cars

Have a rental car reserved and waiting for you upon arival. Fill out all of the paperwork now so you can head right out of the airport into your adventure.

Bus Tickets

You can purchase a bus ticket for the length of your stay that is good for an unlimited number of rides. Regular tickets can be purchased on the island.
